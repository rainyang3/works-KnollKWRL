knoll kwrl





For Unit 16 of DS4, we had to design a brand identity, a short animation , as well as 3 posters for Knoll's research branch KWRL(Knoll Workplace Research Library). Our theme had to be based off of the research paper we chose release by KWRL. My paper was titled " Engaging with Technology."

My idea for the logo was inspired by the idea of a magnifying glass, and the key term "speculative design." This led on from moodboards to a logo system, and then to the idea of making design and graphic connections to blueprints as a tech and futuristic look. I experimented with animation effects to furthur express the idea, and renderings. The third poster has an alternate landscape look that could be used as a billboard design.
